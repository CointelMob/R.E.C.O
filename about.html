About Us

Kelcy Warren

Position : President

Mr. Kelcy Warren is President and Chairman of the Board of Directors of RECO. Mr. Warren has more than 20 years of business experience in the energy industry and currently serves as Chief Executive Officer and Chairman of the Board of Directors of the general partner of Energy Transfer Equity, L. P. (ETE) and also serves in the same capacity with companies affiliated with ETE.



Richard Warren

Position : Vice President

Mr. Richard Warren is General Manager and Vice President of the Board of Directors of RECO. Mr. Warren has over thirty years of experience in the energy industry and has served as senior executive and owner of businesses related to midstream natural gas operations and power generation.



Matthew Harper.

Position : Technical Operations Director and member of the Board.

Mr. Matthew Harper has been associated with the power industry on Roatan and RECO since 1992 when the company was formed as the first user owned utility in Latin America. .He has worked in RECO in various capacities at different points in time ranging from metering department supervisor , line supervisor , operations manager and board member for 5 years prior to his present involvement with KWEI-RECO. A power engineer by profession Mr. Matthew Harper is owner of Green Hill Energy Solutions in Plan Grande and also serves as Her Majesty's Honorary British Consul for the Bay Islands.
